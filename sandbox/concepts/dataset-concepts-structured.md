## Notes for Review

1. Some terms may need standardization (e.g., "two-way ANOVA" vs "ANOVA")
2. Some concepts are very specific (e.g., "coxcomb plot") vs. general (e.g., "time series")
3. Consider whether to include both method names and data type names
4. Some historical terms (e.g., "visualization history") may or may not be appropriate
5. Decide on capitalization standards (e.g., "HE plot" vs "heplot")
